What is Mathfront?

Mathfront is a website where you can practice multiplication and division. How?

Step 1: Mathfront gives you a fully randomized, simple equation.

Step 2: Four multiple choice answers are given, one of which is the correct answer.

Step 3: Do mental math or calculate on paper, and if you get it right, ta-da!
